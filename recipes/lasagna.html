Lasagna

Description

Making homemade lasagna much easier than it looks. You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below!

Allow the homemade lasagna to cool completely, then transfer the leftovers to an airtight container. Store in the refrigerator for up to four days. Reheat in the oven or in the microwave. If you plan to freeze lasagna, it's best to bake it in a foil pan. Allow it to cool completely, then tightly wrap the entire dish in storage wrap. Next, wrap it in at least one tight layer of aluminum foil. Freeze for up to three months. Thaw in the fridge overnight and reheat in the oven or microwave.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • More ingredients :)

  3. Bake in the oven!
